About

SevenBooks Is A startup to help Students Sell And Buy Their Text Books Online. Indeed It is A school focused project but not limited to it. Any person can sell Or Buy a book if it is available on the platform.

Target Audiences

  • School Students
  • Book Worms
  • College students and Professionals

Advantages Over Market

  • First Ever Such Platform In State.
  • More Than 50% Of Students prefer used Books.
  • Nearly Every Student Wants To Get Rid Of Old Books By selling It

Our Challanges

  • We need a considerable amount of Advertisement.
  • The Business Is Somewhat seasonal. It slows Down in non-academic months

Facing Challanges

  • As for Advertisement we will start from local schools and their students. Advertise out Platform to them and request them to give us a try.
  • To Solve the second problem we will promote buying and selling of Books non-acedmics books by professionals and book-worms. this will help us maintaing the company's profit throughout the year.

Money Making
and Business Model

  • Our Primary way of Making money will be By Selling the bought books and the profit we recieve fom it.
  • Once a seller sells a book, we will bind it, label it with our labels and store them. When any costumer orders for the same book we will sell it and will make 10-20% profit, depending on the quality, demand, and condition of book.
  • The best thing about this Model is that it is repeatable. the cycle repeats itself every year, increasing the chances of survival of startup.
